What These Buttons Mean

Before You Enter A Trade

Decide these 3 things first:

Entry: the price where you open the trade.

Target: the price where you take profit if the move goes your way.

Stop Loss: the price where you exit if the idea is wrong.

Simple rule: do not enter first and decide later. Plan entry, target, and stop loss before clicking Buy or Sell.
